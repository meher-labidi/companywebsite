Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize industrial automation in the HVAC and refrigeration industry, ControlPro has grown into a trusted partner for commercial and industrial automation solutions.

Our journey began with a focus on bringing intelligent control systems to the refrigeration and HVAC sector. Today, we've successfully implemented automation solutions for supermarkets, cold storage facilities, commercial buildings, and industrial processes across North America.
